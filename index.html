I'm a PhD student at the University of Chinese Academy of Science, majoring in environmental science. I have research experience at Southern University of Science and Technology, with work experience at Netease Co., Ltd.

I'm interested in environmental modelling and optimal spatial configuration problems. For example, if we know the complex interaction mechanisms between multi-type land use patterns and fine-grained urban thermal environments, what can we say about the spatiotemporal evolution rules of specific thermal fields? How to decompose the multi-coupled environmental system into interpretable modules in a meaningful way? It turns out that such problems are also closely related to pollution control, microclimate regulation, sustainable land spatial planning, and multi-objective environmental tradeoff analysis, and I'm deeply interested in these interdisciplinary connections.
